Virtual Adaptive Yoga

Join us for an at home practice of yoga for folks with disabilities, are wheelchair users, or are interested in exploring their meditative yoga practice from their seat.

The benefits of an adaptive yoga practice helps to decrease stress and anxiety, decrease muscle spasms, decrease pain, improve posture, and increases strength, flexibility, and balance.
